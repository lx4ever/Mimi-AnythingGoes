# Teach Dad ChatGPT for Information Search, English Learning, and General Knowledge

## Idea
I want to teach my dad how to use ChatGPT to search information, learn English, and learn other kinds of knowledge.

## Why this matters
- Family-level AI literacy has practical daily impact.
- The key behavior is not one-shot prompting, but iterative follow-up until understanding.
- A beginner-friendly system can reduce fear and increase confidence quickly.

## Practical structure
1. One core mindset: treat ChatGPT as a patient tutor.
2. One universal prompt template for simple explanations.
3. Three starter scenarios:
   - Information lookup
   - English practice
   - New knowledge learning
4. Follow-up prompts to simplify/explain with examples.
5. Safety checks for medical/legal/financial info and privacy.

## Draft insight
The real barrier is not "using AI" — it is learning to keep asking until it becomes clear.
