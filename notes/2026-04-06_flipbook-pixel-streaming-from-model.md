# Flipbook — 像素级 AI 渲染，无 HTML 无代码

**链接**：https://flipbook.page
**团队**：@eddiejiao_obj, @drewocarr 等

## 内容

Imagine every pixel on your screen, streamed live directly from a model. No HTML, no layout engine, no code. Just exactly what you want to see.

@eddiejiao_obj, @drewocarr and I built a prototype to see how this could actually work, and set out to make it real. We're calling it Flipbook.

## Bubble 的洞察

- 这是 UI 范式的一次根本性挑战：如果模型可以直接生成像素，那"前端"这个概念本身就在被重新定义。你今天存了 AI Engineer 路线图、多 AI 工作流、CRM 未来——Flipbook 是同一个趋势的最极端版本：AI 直接接管人机界面层。
