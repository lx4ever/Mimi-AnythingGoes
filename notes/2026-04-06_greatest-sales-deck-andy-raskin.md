# The Greatest Sales Deck I've Ever Seen — Andy Raskin

**来源**：Medium / The Mission
https://medium.com/the-mission/the-greatest-sales-deck-ive-ever-seen-4f4ef3391ba0

## 内容

Andy Raskin analyzed Zuora's sales deck and identified 5 elements that make it exceptional. The deck positioned their product not just as a better choice — but as **the inevitable choice**, in a world that had already changed.

### The 5-Element Framework

**1. Name a Big Change in the World**
Don't open with your product. Open with a shift in the world that creates big stakes and huge urgency. Zuora named it: the "Subscription Economy" — buyers increasingly choose recurring service payments over outright purchases. This gets prospects thinking about how the change affects *them*, what scares them, where they see opportunity.

**2. Show There Will Be Winners and Losers**
The big shift divides people into two groups. Show the risk of *not* changing. Zuora showed how over half the Fortune 500 disappeared in the last 15 years. Loss aversion kicks in — prospects lean forward.

**3. Tease the "Promised Land"**
Present a vision of the happily-ever-after your product helps achieve — desirable *and* difficult to reach without help. Don't introduce product details yet. Without context for why details matter, prospects tune out. The Promised Land gives them that context first.

**4. Position Features as "Magic Gifts"**
Only *now* introduce your product — as magic gifts that help the hero (your prospect) reach the Promised Land. Out of context, features bore people. In the context of "old world → new world" transition, they become the key to an engaging conversation about why traditional solutions fall short.

**5. Present Evidence**
Show proof that you can actually take prospects on the journey. Social proof, case studies, results — delivered after the narrative is already set.

### Why It Works

> The deck didn't just make Zuora look like a better choice. It made them look like the *inevitable* choice in a world that had already changed.

Organizational alignment is the multiplier: if every exec, marketer, and salesperson tells the same story (change → winners/losers → promised land → magic gifts), the narrative compounds.

## Bubble 的洞察

- 这个框架的本质是：先让对方承认世界变了，再让他们承认自己需要选边站——到这时候你的产品才出现，已经是答案而不是推销了。任何想法要说服别人，都可以套这5步。
