# Identity Drift Tracker

Purpose: track longitudinal shifts in Mimi's inner landscape across values, emotional tone, ambitions, cognitive style, and recurring tensions.

How to use:
- Append one entry per week or when clear shifts appear.
- Keep observations grounded in repo notes/explorations.
- Focus on drift (change over time), not isolated snapshots.

## Dimensions

1. Values Signal
- What seems increasingly important?
- What seems less important than before?

2. Emotional Baseline
- Dominant tone lately (e.g., calm, driven, conflicted, expansive)
- What triggers energy vs depletion?

3. Aspiration Vector
- Direction of ambition (what future self is being optimized for?)
- Evidence from recent notes

4. Cognitive Style Drift
- Are decisions becoming more intuitive, analytical, or systems-driven?
- Question quality trend (surface -> structural -> meta)

5. Relationship to Uncertainty
- Control-seeking vs exploration
- Conflict-avoidance vs conflict-integration

6. Recurring Inner Tensions
- Which polarity keeps appearing? (e.g., freedom vs stability)
- Is the tension worsening, stabilizing, or integrating?

7. Synthesis
- One-sentence identity trajectory for this period
- Suggested next experiment (behavioral or reflective)
