# Belief Rewrite Card

Use when you feel stuck, doubtful, or caught in an old pattern.

## Core Prompt

In the past, I used to believe that **_____**.
Now, I choose to believe that **_____**.
So from today, I will **_____**.

---

## Fast Version (60 seconds)

1. Old belief: _____
2. New belief: _____
3. Next micro-action (within 10 minutes): _____

---

## Deeper Version (5 minutes)

### 1) Old Script
- What did I believe before?
- Where did this belief come from?

### 2) New Script
- What do I choose to believe now?
- Why is this belief more aligned with who I’m becoming?

### 3) Behavioral Proof
- What one action will prove this new belief today?
- What one action will prove it this week?

---

## Example

In the past, I used to believe that I need to feel ready before I act.
Now, I choose to believe that action creates readiness.
So from today, I will take one imperfect step before overthinking.

---

## Weekly Reflection

- Which old belief showed up most this week?
- Which new belief did I actually live?
- What action created the strongest shift?
